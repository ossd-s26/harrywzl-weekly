## presentations this week
Out of all the presentations this week, the two that stuck with me the most were Keycloak and Nanobot. Keycloak is backed by Red Hat, and the group made contributions that helped the people using the project. Ben fixed a dropdown bar issue, and the PR that Jerry and Sebastian submitted showed that they understood the codebase. In the Nanobot presentation, one of their group members made a point that you should not contribute what you want to contribute, you should contribute what the maintainer and the community want. For our group, most of us have been going back and forth with maintainers and waiting on reviews.
