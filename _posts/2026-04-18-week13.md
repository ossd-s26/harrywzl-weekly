## contribution
This week I made a contribution to pandas that improves one of its core components -> apply.
I removed an old pattern that changed internal state during aggregation. This makes the code easier to understand and reduces the chance of bugs when exceptions occur.
The same change also fixed a groupby aggregation that previously returned the wrong result type.
I learned that small refactors in widely used libraries can affect many users and that testing decisions matter.
The maintainer gave clear feedback early, which helped me consider the project’s testing approach and improved the final result through collaboration.
